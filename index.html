The Creative Process

Research is a multi step process that begins with analysing competition as well as any data such as clicks, page views, and performance. User feedback is also crucial during this step in order to understand how to build an experience that addresses our user needs.

Exploration. During this phase of the process we begin to build out a prototype. Sketching out our ideas, brainstorming, and later adding structure to these ideas. It is imperative to keep in mind the culture of the user, and well as staying true to the brand personality.

Analysis. At this point, the service or website will be live and available for use. Now we can begin measuring how well we are doing. Qualitative methods are much better at answering why or how we can fix a problem.
